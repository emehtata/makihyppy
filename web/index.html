Controls
  • Space — start the approach run, then press again at the right moment to take off.
  • A / D — raise or lower the skis while airborne. This changes the original flight stance, including its risk and scoring effects.
  • Land in stance 1 or 2 for a clean landing. Stance 3 or 4 falls; pushing beyond either end also falls.
